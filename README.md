## ⚙️ Methodology  
1. 🔄 ETL Process  
   - Extracted raw leave data from organisational systems.  
   - Transformed data to remove duplicates, standardise categories, and validate anomalies.  
   - Loaded cleaned dataset into Excel and SPSS for structured analysis.  

2. 📊 Analytical Techniques  
   - Descriptive statistics for workforce profiling.  
   - Trend analysis (2022–2024 leave utilisation).  
   - Inferential statistics (ANOVA, regression) for absenteeism predictors.  
   - Visualisations (heatmaps, scatter plots, dashboards).  

3. 🖥️ Tools Used  
   - 🐍 Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn) – ETL automation, anomaly detection, visualisations.  
   - 📈 IBM SPSS – statistical validation (ANOVA, regression).  
   - 📊 Microsoft Excel – descriptive statistics, tabulation.  
   - 🌐 GitHub – version‑controlled repository for scripts, datasets, and outputs.  

---

## 📊 Key Findings  
- 🩺 Sick leave is the dominant category across all departments.  
- 👩‍👩‍👧 Female employees report higher leave utilisation due to maternity and family responsibility.  
- 📉 Leave utilisation declined 13% between 2022 and 2024, raising questions about underreporting.  
- ⏱️ 95% of leave captured after the event, undermining payroll accuracy and planning.  
- ❄️ Seasonal spikes (winter sick leave, December annual leave) strain service delivery.  

---

## 🚨 Risks & Implications  
- ⚠️ Service disruptions and overtime costs due to unplanned absences.  
- 💼 Unequal workload distribution leading to fatigue in frontline roles.  
- 🗂️ Data quality issues eroding trust in workforce reporting.  
- 🛡️ Governance gaps reducing decision‑making confidence.  

---

## ✅ Recommendations  
- 📊 Develop a **real‑time workforce analytics dashboard**.  
- ⏳ Achieve **95% leave capture compliance within 5 days**.  
- 📑 Standardise **data governance definitions and validation rules**.  
- 🧘 Launch **employee wellness and mental health programmes**.  
- 🔮 Implement **predictive analytics models** for absence forecasting.  
- 🤖 Automate ETL pipelines and establish **data ownership governance**.
